MLS Soccer Betting Guide

Navigate the growing world of Major League Soccer betting. Learn about draws, low-scoring games, and the unique strategies that make soccer betting exciting.

Why Soccer Betting is Different

Soccer (football globally) offers a unique betting experience. With three possible outcomes (win, lose, draw), lower scoring than other sports, and matches that can end in ties, soccer requires different strategies than traditional American sports betting.

MLS Season Overview

February - November season (34 regular season games)
Single-elimination playoff format
Growing popularity and betting interest
Mix of young American talent and international stars

Understanding Soccer Odds

Soccer betting features three-way moneylines since games can end in draws. This creates different value opportunities compared to two-outcome sports.

3-Way Moneyline Example

LAFC Win: -150
Draw: +240
Portland Timbers Win: +400

You must pick the exact outcome - LAFC win, draw, or Portland win. There's no "push" in soccer betting.

Double Chance Betting

For beginners, double chance bets cover two of the three outcomes:

LAFC or Draw: Wins if LAFC wins or the game ends in a tie
Portland or Draw: Wins if Portland wins or the game ends in a tie
LAFC or Portland: Wins if either team wins (no draw)

Soccer Totals (Goals)

Soccer totals typically range from 2.5 to 3.5 goals, much lower than other sports. Understanding when games will be high or low scoring is crucial.

Total Goals Type of Game Typical Scenario
Over 2.5 Higher scoring Offensive teams, open play
Under 2.5 Lower scoring Defensive teams, cautious play
Over 3.5 High scoring Weak defenses, must-win situations

Factors Affecting Goal Totals

Weather: Rain and wind reduce scoring
Stakes: Playoff games often more cautious
Team Style: Some teams focus on defense, others attack
Missing Players: Key strikers or defenders out

MLS-Specific Betting Considerations

MLS has unique characteristics that create betting opportunities for informed bettors who understand the league's nuances.

MLS Unique Factors

• Designated Player rule creates talent imbalances
• Salary cap with discovery and allocation money
• Single-table standings (no conferences in betting)
• Rapid league expansion and growth
• Mix of playing styles and philosophies

Travel and Geography

MLS teams travel vast distances across multiple time zones. West Coast teams traveling east for early games, or teams playing at altitude in Colorado, can face significant challenges.

Designated Players Impact

Teams with world-class designated players (DPs) like Carlos Vela or Sebastian Giovinco can dramatically outperform their overall team strength in specific games.

Soccer Player Props

Soccer prop betting is growing rapidly, offering opportunities to bet on individual player performances rather than match outcomes.

Anytime Goal Scorer

Will a specific player score at any point in the match? Strikers and attacking midfielders featured.

First Goal Scorer

Who scores the opening goal? Higher odds but requires precise prediction.

Shots on Target

Over/under for player shots that require a save or find the net.

Assists

Playmaker props for creative midfielders and wing players.

Cards Received

Yellow/red card props for aggressive players or heated rivalries.

Saves

Goalkeeper save totals, especially when facing high-volume shooting teams.

Live Soccer Betting

Soccer offers excellent live betting opportunities due to its continuous 90-minute action and momentum shifts that can dramatically change game flow.

Key Live Betting Moments

• After the first goal (changes game dynamics)
• Red card situations (team with advantage)
• Halftime adjustments and substitutions
• Final 15 minutes when teams chase goals
• Extra time in playoff matches

Early Goal Impact

The first goal in soccer often changes the entire match dynamic. Teams that score early may become more defensive, while trailing teams must commit more players forward, potentially creating more goal-scoring opportunities.

Live Betting Example

If a defensive team takes an early 1-0 lead, the total goals line might drop from 2.5 to 2.0, as they're likely to focus on protecting their advantage rather than scoring more.

MLS Season Strategies

The MLS season has distinct phases that create different betting opportunities throughout the year.

Early Season (February - May)

Teams are integrating new players and finding their rhythm. Weather can be unpredictable in northern markets. Focus on teams with continuity from previous seasons.

Summer Soccer (June - August)

Peak season with consistent weather and established team chemistry. International tournaments may impact player availability.

Playoff Push (September - October)

Teams fighting for playoff spots show increased motivation. Some teams may rest players if playoffs are secured or impossible.

MLS Cup Playoffs

Single-elimination format creates unpredictable results. Motivation and experience often outweigh regular season form.

Research and Statistics

Successful soccer betting requires understanding both traditional stats and advanced metrics that better predict future performance.

Key Soccer Statistics

Expected Goals (xG): Quality of scoring chances created
Goals Per Game: Offensive output
Goals Against Per Game: Defensive strength
Possession %: Control of the game
Shots on Target: Quality of attack

Form vs Fixtures

Recent form (last 5-6 games) often matters more than season-long statistics in soccer. However, also consider the quality of opposition faced during that form period.

Home Field Advantage

Soccer has strong home field advantage, especially in MLS where supporter culture is passionate. Some teams perform dramatically better at home vs away.

Bankroll Management for Soccer

Soccer's three-outcome nature and low-scoring games require different bankroll strategies than traditional American sports.

Managing Draw Risk

Draws occur in roughly 25-30% of soccer matches. When betting moneylines, factor in this possibility when calculating expected value.

Value in Lower Leagues

As MLS grows in popularity, consider that less mainstream soccer leagues may offer better betting value due to less sharp money.

Soccer Betting Patience

Soccer betting rewards patience. With fewer games per week than other sports, focus on thoroughly researching the matches you do bet rather than betting frequently.
