Dream vs Mercury Betting Preview: Expert Picks for July 23 WNBA Showdown

Phoenix Mercury host Atlanta Dream tonight at 10 PM ET as 7.5-point favorites. Our expert analysis covers key player props, injury impacts, and strategic betting opportunities for this Western Conference clash.

Current Betting Odds & Lines

Point Spread Mercury -7.5 (-110)
Moneyline Mercury -350 | Dream +280
Total Points Over/Under 168.5
Game Time 10:00 PM ET

Team Analysis & Current Form

Phoenix Mercury (15-7) - Heating Up

The Mercury enter tonight's contest as significant home favorites, and for good reason. Phoenix sits in third place in the WNBA standings with a 15-7 record, showcasing their strength throughout the 2025 season.

Mercury's Big Three Finally Healthy

The key storyline for Phoenix is the return to health of their core trio: Alyssa Thomas, Satou Sabally, and Kahleah Copper. This trio has played just six games together this season due to various injuries, but when healthy, they've been dominant with a +4.9 net rating.

Atlanta Dream (13-10) - Cooling Off

The Dream have struggled recently, losing six of their past nine games and dropping to fifth place in the standings at 13-10. This recent slide has hurt their playoff positioning as they fight to secure a favorable seed.

Tonight's game represents a critical opportunity for Atlanta on the road, but they'll be facing a motivated Mercury team looking to solidify their playoff position.

Key Players to Watch

Alyssa Thomas (Phoenix Mercury) - Heating Up

Assists Prop: Over 9.5 assists (-110) - Thomas has been the engine of Phoenix's offense, averaging 10.8 assists in the six games where the big three played together. She cleared this prop in four of those six contests.

Kahleah Copper (Phoenix Mercury) - Return from Injury

3-Pointers Prop: Over 1.5 made (-115) - Copper returns tonight after missing time with a hamstring injury. She's shooting an impressive 44.8% from three-point range this season, her highest mark since her rookie year.

Satou Sabally (Phoenix Mercury) - Rebounds Focus

Rebounds Prop: Over 6.5 rebounds (-105) - Sabally has been a consistent contributor on the boards and should see increased opportunities with Phoenix's uptempo style.

Allisha Gray (Atlanta Dream) - Stepping Up

Rebounds Prop: Over 6.5 rebounds (+110) - With Rhyne Howard out due to knee injury, Gray has significantly increased her rebounding. She's averaged 8.5 rebounds in games without Howard compared to 5.1 when he plays.

Injury Report & Impact

Atlanta Dream

Rhyne Howard (OUT) - Knee injury. Howard's absence continues to impact Atlanta's offensive flow and puts additional pressure on other players to step up, particularly in the rebounding department.

Phoenix Mercury

Monique Akoa Makani (OUT) - Concussion protocol. The young guard's absence creates opportunity for other bench players to contribute.

Expert Betting Analysis

Why Mercury -7.5 Makes Sense

The spread reflects Phoenix's home court advantage and their recent strong play. With their core trio finally healthy and playing together, the Mercury have shown they can be dominant on both ends of the floor. Their offensive rating of 103.4 with defensive rating of 98.5 creates a strong foundation for covering larger spreads.

Total Points Considerations

The 168.5 total appears reasonable given both teams' recent scoring patterns. Phoenix's uptempo style with their big three healthy could push this over, especially if Atlanta struggles on the second night of a back-to-back situation.

Player Props Value

The Alyssa Thomas assists prop at 9.5 offers strong value given her recent performance with the full lineup healthy. Meanwhile, Allisha Gray's rebounding prop benefits from increased usage with Howard sidelined.
