What is a Betting Teaser?

Teaser Basics

A teaser lets you adjust point spreads and totals in your favor. You get better lines, but lower payouts than regular parlays.

Think of it as: Buying points to make your bets easier to win!

How It Works

Choose 2+ bets and pick your teaser amount (6 points, 6.5 points, etc). Every spread and total moves in your favor by that amount.

Example: Cowboys -7 becomes Cowboys -1 in a 6-point teaser!

Why Use Teasers

Teasers offer a smart middle ground - better winning chances than regular parlays, but higher payouts than single bets.

Perfect for: Conservative bettors who want parlay upside with less risk.

Betting Teaser Calculator FAQ

What sports can I use teasers on?

Teasers are most commonly used on NFL and NBA games. You can also bet college football and college basketball teasers. Our calculator supports all four sports.

How many points should I tease?

For NFL, 6-point teasers are most popular. For NBA, 4-point teasers are standard. More points make winning easier but reduce payouts.

What's the minimum number of bets for a teaser?

You need at least 2 bets to create a teaser. Most sportsbooks allow up to 6-8 bets in a teaser.

Are teasers better than parlays?

Teasers have higher win rates than parlays because the lines move in your favor, but lower payouts. They're good for conservative bettors who want parlay-style upside with less risk.
